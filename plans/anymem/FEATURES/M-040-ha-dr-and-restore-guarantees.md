# M-040 - HA/DR and Restore Guarantees

## Status
- Priority: `P1`
- Phase: `2 - Broker + Tooling`
- State: `Planned`

## Goal
Define and validate high-availability and disaster-recovery guarantees suitable for enterprise deployment.

## Acceptance Criteria
- RPO/RTO targets are explicit per deployment class.
- Backup and restore procedures are automated and tested.
- Disaster-recovery drills are scheduled and evidence is retained.
- Multi-region or equivalent resilience strategy is defined.
- Incident response runbooks are linked to restore and failover paths.

## Dependencies
- `M-002`, `M-005`, `M-028`, `M-032`
